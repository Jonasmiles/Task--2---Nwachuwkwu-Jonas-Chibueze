## Key Findings



### Payment Method Analysis



* Credit Card generated the highest profit.

* Online payments recorded the highest order volume.

* Debit Card transactions produced the lowest profit among payment options.



### Product Performance



* Chair generated the highest profit.

* Printer and Laptop products also performed strongly.

* Phone generated the lowest profit among analyzed products.



### Referral Source Analysis



* Instagram generated the highest sales revenue and profit.

* Email marketing delivered strong performance.

* Referral traffic produced the lowest profit contribution.



### Coupon Usage Analysis



* FREESHIP generated the highest sales and profit.

* SAVE10 and WINTER15 produced lower overall performance.

* Orders without coupons still accounted for a significant share of revenue.



### Order Status Analysis



* Delivered and Shipped orders contributed significantly to revenue.

* Returned orders reduced overall profitability.

* Monitoring return rates could improve future performance.



## Trends Identified



* Digital channels such as Instagram and Email drive strong sales performance.

* Customers frequently utilize shipping-related promotions.

* Online and card-based payment methods dominate purchasing behavior.

* High-performing products consistently contribute the largest share of profit.



## Outlier Analysis



Outliers were identified using the IQR method. These records represent unusually high transaction values and may indicate:



* Bulk purchases

* Corporate orders

* Data entry anomalies

* High-value customers



These observations were retained because they may contain valuable business insights.



## Business Recommendations



1. Increase investment in Instagram and Email marketing campaigns.

2. Expand promotions similar to the FREESHIP coupon strategy.

3. Focus inventory planning around high-performing products.

4. Investigate reasons for product returns to reduce revenue loss.

5. Develop loyalty programs targeting high-value customers.
